Frequently Asked

Will AI replace my existing team? +
No. Our agents are "Digital Employees" that handle the robotic, repetitive tasks, allowing your human team to focus on high-level strategy and relationships.
What is RAG and how does it help? +
RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) allows your AI to access specific documents (PDFs, training manuals, Notion) to provide accurate answers without making things up.
Can voice agents handle accents? +
Yes. Our voice agents are hyper-realistic and can be trained on specific accents and industry jargon to ensure a seamless customer experience.
